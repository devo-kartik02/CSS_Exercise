Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 slice of cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat a grill to medium heat.
  2. Spread a thin layer of butter on each slice of bread.
  3. Place the bacon slices on the grill and cook until crispy.
  4. Place the tomato slices on top of the bacon.
  5. Place the cheese slice on top of the tomato slices.
  6. Close the sandwich by placing the second slice of bread on top.
  7. Grill the sandwich for 2-3 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the bread is golden brown.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
